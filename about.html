About Us

Founded in 2003 by its Founder and Managing Partner, Ms Tan Lee Kiew, Tan Lee Kiew & Co is a boutique SCA (Singapore Chartered Accountant) firm in Singapore, offering a complete suite of services to businesses audit, accounting, tax (including corporate tax, personal income tax and Goods & Services Tax). Through its associated firm, Magdalene Management Services Pte Ltd, we also provide services for incorporation of companies and corporate secretarial services.

The firm started off from a sole practice of Ms Tan in East Coast Road and in the short space of 3 years, has grown to a 10-auditor SCA (Singapore Chartered Accountant) firm with a large and diverse client base, ranging from Singapore-based trading and manufacturing companies, to law firms, to European investment corporations.

Tan Lee Kiew & Co prides itself on the consistently high quality of its work, its responsiveness to clients' needs and its quick turnaround. At the commencement of each engagement, our professionals will spend time listening to, and understanding, clients' objectives and commercial needs, thus ensuring that the assignment is carried out efficiently and cost-effectively. All of our professionals are well-trained and carefully supervised in their work. In addition, they regularly upgrade their knowledge and keep abreast of cutting-edge developments in accounting standards, by participating in conferences and seminars.

The size of our firm makes it possible for our professionals to maintain close attention to each and every engagement that we take on, as well as remain approachable to our clients. Tan Lee Kiew & Co also works with a network of law firms, valuers and other relevant professionals to provide a seamless service to our clients.
