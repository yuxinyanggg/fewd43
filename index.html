About Us

Founded in 2003 by its Managing Partner, Ms Tan Lee Kiew, Tan Lee Kiew & Co. is a boutique SCA (Singapore Chartered Accountancy) firm offering a complete suite of services to businesses, in:

  • audit
  • accounting
  • tax (corporate tax, personal income tax and Goods & Services Tax)

Through its associated firm, Magdalene Management Services Pte Ltd, we also provide services for incorporation of companies and corporate secretarial services.

The firm started off from Ms Tan's sole practice in East Coast Road and has since grown to a 10-auditor SCA (Singapore Chartered Accountant) firm with a large and diverse client base, ranging from Singapore-based trading and manufacturing companies, to law firms as well as European investment corporations.

Tan Lee Kiew & Co. prides itself on the consistently high quality of its work, its responsiveness to clients' needs and its quick turnaround. At the commencement of each engagement, our professionals will spend time listening to, and understanding, clients' objectives and commercial needs, ensuring that the assignment is carried out efficiently and cost-effectively. All of our professionals are well-trained and carefully supervised in their work. In addition, they regularly upgrade their knowledge and keep abreast of cutting-edge developments in accounting standards, by participating in conferences and seminars.

The size of our firm makes it possible for our professionals to maintain close attention to each and every engagement that we take on, as well as remain approachable to our clients. Tan Lee Kiew & Co. also works with a network of law firms, valuers and other relevant professionals to provide a seamless service to our clients.

Our Services

Our Services

For all organisations, accounts preparation on an annual basis is essential to the effective running of their business. In the case of Companies, there is a statutory obligation to file audited accounts in a statutory format annually with ACRA. For Sole Proprietors and Partnerships, the information obtained from annual accounts preparation not only helps you measure your business performance, it also provides financial information required for the completion of personal tax returns.

We not only seek to help businesses fulfill their statutory obligations, but also use the opportunity to work with the company's management team to improve their overall business performance. Monitoring the financial performance of your business is of paramount importance and regular management accounting information is vital to the continued success of your business. We can undertake the management accounting responsibility on your behalf and provide regular financial reports and advisory services to help you improve your profits or to help your business grow.

Our Accounting & Audit Services include:

  • Accounts Preparation & Statutory Filing
  • Bookkeeping
  • Audit
  • Accounting Advisory
